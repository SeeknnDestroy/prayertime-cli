# Code of Conduct

## Expectations

- Be respectful, direct, and technically rigorous.
- Assume good faith, but prioritize clarity and evidence over rhetoric.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project for users and contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Bad-faith contributions, spam, or deliberately disruptive conduct.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the health of the project.
